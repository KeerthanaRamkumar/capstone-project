About Us

RK Hospital was established in 1995 with a vision to provide affordable and world-class healthcare to the community. Our hospital is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities, highly skilled doctors, caring nurses, and modern technology to ensure the best treatment for our patients.

We specialize in cardiology, neurology, pediatrics, surgery, and emergency care. With more than 25 years of service, RK Hospital continues to serve with compassion, dedication, and trust.
